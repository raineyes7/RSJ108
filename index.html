How to Brainstorm ...

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Have you ever been stuck when it comes to coming up with ideas for writing an aritcle? Here are a few activites and solutions to get those brain juices flowing.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Pen
  • Paper
  • Accese to Research
  • Accese to Internet
  • Recording Device
  • Conversation Skills

  1. Look at Current Events Around You.

    Journalism is a field that is very importnat when it comes to providing information on current events. Therefore, take some time to research current events in the world and your area. Take notes on what stands out to you.

  2. Find Your Main Audience.

    If you can identify who your main audience is that you are writing for, you are halfway there for writing a great article. Look at past pieces you have written and try to identify your main auidence so you can narrow down what ideas are going to be important to them.

  3. Have a Conversation.

    Once you have identified your main auidence you can take it a step further by having a conversation with them. The ablity to conversate and interview is a great tool to have as a Journalist.

  4. Focus on Important People.

    If those other steps have not given you the results you wanted you can always look at impactful people, espically in your community. Writing a feature story is always a solid article idea.

  5. Write About an Organization.

    When the ideas are not coming to mind, it could always be a good time to write an article about an Orgnaization. You can research organizations within your community and highlight what they are bringing to the community. Take it a step further by looking in to events that organizations may be putting on.
